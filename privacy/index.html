Privacy Policy – aroMathiz

Last updated: April 20, 2026

Introduction

The aroMathiz application places great importance on user privacy and data protection.

This privacy policy explains what data may be collected, for what purpose, and your rights.

Data Collected

aroMathiz does not collect any personal data that can directly identify users.

However, the app may collect anonymous technical data through Firebase Crashlytics.

  • crash reports
  • device information (model, OS version)
  • usage data in case of errors

Purpose of Data Collection

  • improve app stability
  • detect and fix bugs
  • enhance user experience

Consent

On first launch, you can accept or refuse anonymous data collection.

  • If accepted: anonymous reports may be sent
  • If refused: no data is collected

This choice does not affect app functionality.

Withdrawal of Consent

You can withdraw your consent by uninstalling and reinstalling the app.

Data Sharing

No data is sold or shared.

Anonymous data is processed by Firebase Crashlytics, a Google service.

Security

Data is handled securely via Firebase services.
