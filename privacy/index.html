Privacy Policy – aroMathiz

Last updated: April 21, 2026

Introduction

The aroMathiz application places great importance on user privacy and data protection.

This privacy policy explains what data may be collected, for what purpose, and your rights.

Data Collected

The application may collect certain data automatically through third-party services integrated into the app.

  • crash reports and diagnostic data
  • device information (such as model and operating system version)
  • app usage data in case of errors
  • advertising identifiers, IP address, and ad interaction data
  • purchase-related technical data for in-app purchases

This data is used only for app functionality, analytics, stability, advertising, and purchase management. It may not directly identify you, but some of it may be considered personal data under applicable laws.

Purpose of Data Collection

  • improve app stability
  • detect and fix bugs
  • enhance user experience
  • display rewarded advertisements
  • manage in-app purchases

Advertising

The app uses Google AdMob to display advertisements, including rewarded video ads.

Google may collect and process certain data such as advertising identifiers, IP address, device information, and interactions with ads in order to provide, measure, and improve advertising services.

You can learn more about how Google uses data here:

https://policies.google.com/privacy

You can manage your advertising preferences here:

https://adssettings.google.com

In-App Purchases

The app offers in-app purchases, including the purchase of tokens.

All payments are securely processed by Google Play Billing.

The application does not collect or store your payment card or banking information.

Consent

On first launch, you may be asked to make choices regarding certain data collection features where required by applicable laws.

Some data may still be processed when necessary for core app functionality, crash reporting, purchase management, or ad delivery.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data.

However, some data may be processed by third-party services used in the application, including Google services such as Firebase Crashlytics, Google AdMob, and Google Play Billing.

Third-Party Services

  • Firebase Crashlytics (crash reporting and diagnostics)
  • Google AdMob (advertising)
  • Google Play Billing (in-app purchases)

Security

Reasonable measures are taken to protect data processed through the application and its service providers.

Your Rights

Depending on your country or region, you may have rights regarding access to, deletion of, or restriction of certain personal data processed by third-party services.
